Hi, Myeongdong office workers! I'm a 40-something editor who lives for lunchtime.

Today, I finally went to this tteokbokki place that everyone's been talking about.

The star of the show is the tteokbokki place famous for 'Garlic Gopchang Tteokbokki'. This place has the pre-payment system that's common in Myeongdong.

The signature menu is, of course, Garlic Gopchang Tteokbokki!

The garlic smell is seriously amazing. The gopchang (small intestines) is chewy and goes so well with the tteokbokki sauce. The tteok (rice cake) is long, and it's fun to cut it yourself. You can even eat a whole strand if you want. I really loved the chewy texture of the tteok.

The price is a bit high for tteokbokki, but considering it has gopchang and the portion is pretty big, I think it's worth it. The atmosphere is like a trendy tteokbokki place. It's perfect for hanging out and chatting with friends.

If you're looking for a good tteokbokki place in Myeongdong, I totally recommend this place! Make sure to try the Garlic Gopchang Tteokbokki. You won't regret it!

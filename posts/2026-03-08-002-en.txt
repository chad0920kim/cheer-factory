Hi everyone, your Myeongdong office worker food explorer here!

Today I'm gonna introduce a dak han mari (Korean chicken stew) place with a really amazing, spicy, and refreshing broth. They also sell chicken salad (dak muchim) and chicken soup (dak gomtang), so it's great to pick whatever you like!

These days, when the weather's chilly, you crave some hot soup, right? The broth here is seriously the real deal, and it makes you feel like you're getting a boost of energy. And the price is reasonable, so you can enjoy it without worrying about your wallet.

Isn't adding a ton of garlic to dak han mari like, a must-do? I absolutely love garlic, so I added a lot! And you all know that a sprinkle of pepper really brings out the flavor, right?

The side dishes are also neat and tidy, and guess what? They give you baby octopus kimchi (nakji jeotgal)! Giving expensive nakji jeotgal as a side dish? The owner's generosity is the best!

I'm leaving with a full stomach again today. If you're craving dak han mari in Myeongdong, I highly recommend this place!
